How to Play Tennis

Tennis is a strategic, fast-paced sport played in singles or doubles. The aim: hit the ball over the net so your opponent can't return it cleanly.

  • Each point begins with a serve—diagonally into the opposite service box.
  • A legal return must land within bounds, or you win the point.
  • Games are counted in points—Love (0), 15, 30, 40, then Game.
  • At 40–40, players must win by two points (Advantage system).

Core Rules of Tennis

  1. A match starts with a coin toss to decide who serves first.
  2. Players switch ends after every odd-numbered game.
  3. The serve must land in the correct service box; two faults equate to a point loss.
  4. Balls hitting the line are considered “in.”
