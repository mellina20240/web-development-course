History of Tennis

Tennis dates back to 12th century France where it was played with the palm of the hand. The game evolved, and by the 19th century, lawn tennis emerged in England as a racquet sport. Today, tennis is a global sport with grand slam tournaments like Wimbledon, the US Open, and the French Open attracting millions of fans.

The professional era began in the 1960s, leading to today's ATP and WTA tours. Legends like Rod Laver, Martina Navratilova, Pete Sampras, and Serena Williams have shaped the sport into what it is today.
