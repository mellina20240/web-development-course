What do hamsters like ?

Hamsters enjoy a varied diet including seeds, nuts, grains, fruits, and vegetables. They also appreciate hamster-specific treats, and enjoy chewing on wood, cardboard, or chew toys to keep their teeth healthy. Furthermore, hamsters thrive with opportunities for play, such as running in a wheel, exploring tunnels, and hiding in various enclosures.

Food:

Hamsters are omnivores and enjoy a varied diet. This includes a high-quality seed mix with high-protein pellets, supplemented with fresh fruits and vegetables like carrots, broccoli, spinach, apples, and berries.

Chew Toys:

Hamsters have a natural urge to chew, and providing them with wood chews, cardboard tubes, or specially designed rodent chew toys helps keep their teeth healthy and provides enrichment.
